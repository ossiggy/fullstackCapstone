Budge My Life is an app meant to simplify the creation of your budget. We've cut down the process to five important steps: how much you make, how much you have in your account, how much you have in bills, how much you want to save, and if you've paid yet. Start by entering your income, whether it be weekly, bi-weekly, monthly or whenever you get paid. Then, enter your current funds into the "Total Remaining Funds" section. After that, hit "create" under either expenses or savings to add a row and get started! Once you have a category, simply hit "paid" when you've paid the bill for your remaining funds to update. Make a mistake? No problem! The undo button will rebalance your budget to where you were before you hit "paid". Happy Saving!
